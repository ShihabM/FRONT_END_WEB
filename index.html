Milkeddit

This beautiful reddit client was designed with users in mind. Every single action and reaction is fluid and a pleasure to use.

Milkeddit is a feature-rich and fluid reddit client that makes using reddit a joy for both casual and experienced users alike. With support for multiple accounts, 3D Touch, multireddits, mute filters, an in-app image and gif viewer, a dedicated gallery mode, full-screen browsing, and a plethora of customisation options, Milkeddit has everything you'll expect from reddit, and much more which you will come to love. "Pictures don't do it justice." Select your preferred layout for text and thumbnails, and choose from a set of gorgeous themes, which includes night mode, to help you read better in the later hours. Have complete control over metadata, and toggle the hiding of voted and read posts. See how long posts take to read before reading them, search across reddit, and mute certain words or phrases from ever popping up onto your feed. Filter the content, subscribe and bookmark subreddits for later viewing, send posts to your favourite 'read later' service, and watch how Milkeddit becomes personalised to you. The gorgeous layout, along with the swift and silky navigation and refresh mechanisms allow for a seamlessly beautiful and joyous experience app-wide. Not only is Milkeddit a complete solution for your reddit needs, but it is also an enjoyable one.

Scavenger

This beautiful location-based social network was designed with users in mind. Every single action and reaction is fluid and a pleasure to use.

Scavenger is an anonymous location-based social network that allows you to get to know your surroundings like never before. Express your thoughts, express yourself, and exchange views. See what's around you, and get to know your area a bit better. Scavenger lets you explore your local community from a side you've never seen before.

Scavenger lets you post anonymously within a 200 character limit per post and reply. These posts expire in 24 hours, so as not to overpopulate your local area. The ability to upload pictures and maybe even short video clips will be coming soon in a future update, along with reply chains. Scavenger is now available on the AppStore for free. Download it now and give it a go. Post messages anonymously, reply to others, and view your area like never before. The possibilities are endless, go out and explore your area.

From the module perspective, the site was created using all three required languages (HTML/CSS/JavaScript), which is predominantly hand-written code and responsive. The menu bar is displayed with a hamburger icon for mobile devices, and the other areas appropriately resize and wrap around in accordance with the screen size. The site maakes use of Twitter's API for the social area which displays a couple of tweets. Advanced CSS3 and JavaScript is used for mainly for the header section, where rotating shapes at different speeds give the wave-like effect, and is also employed for the parallax effect with the mountains.

The site is structured as a single-page site, with easy to navigate content that is simple enough to locate. It also makes use of flexbox for the footer area. The portfolio section is divided into sub-categories which are displayed as a dropdown menu on the menu bar. This site is accessible.
